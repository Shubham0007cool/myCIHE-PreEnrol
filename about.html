About CIHE Pre-Enrollment system

CIHE pre-enrolment system is an astounding idea of website that solely is for the enhancement of the quality of enrolment for the CIHE students and administration. With having large number of students every year from all over the world in both ACT campus and NSW campus, it has been a task for someone to take a lead and work to make a remarkable example of educational institution. Having approximately 4000 students for each year, it has been always a problem for CIHE to manage the student enrolments, managing classes, lecturers and timetable. Besides this, respective course co-coordinators use their excel sheet to assign(recommend) units to students. And this creates an extra work for coordinators and administrators. So, to tackle this issue, we have decided to develop CIHE pre-enrolment website that helps its students to register themselves for enrolment (pre-enrolment), and its administrators to manage the student numbers, lecturers and classes

Our advantages

CIHE pre-enrolment website awaits to provide the CIHE students a reliable platform for students and administration to register and access application for the registration of enrolment. With the development of the website, we expect to track the number of students, teachers and classes to assign for the enrolment period. We expect to provide exact data to the admin about the number of students and that they will be studying and limit the number of students for the specific course they can register to enrol. This website must include security feature like trouble shooting and aim to provide the best UI/UX to the users, including several other features simply to fulfill the requirements and to make the pre-enrolment effective and efficient.

CIHE offers various scholarships to support students in their academic journey. These financial aids are designed to recognize excellence and help students achieve their educational goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for a course?

Navigate to the Subject Registration page and follow the steps provided.

Can I change my password?

Yes, click on "Change Password" under the Settings menu to update your credentials.

What is the deadline for course registration?

Course registration deadlines are displayed on your dashboard and sent via email notifications. Ensure you register before the specified date.

How can I contact support if I encounter an issue?

You can reach our support team through the "Support" page or by emailing us at support@cihe.com.

Can I edit my registered courses?

Yes, you can edit your registered courses before the registration deadline. Go to the "Subject Registration" page and modify your selection.

How do I update my personal information?

To update your personal details, go to the "Profile" section, and click on "Edit Profile" to make the necessary changes
